FAQ

Questions, answered

How much does beta cost?
Nothing. Beta access is completely free. Every business that joins also receives S$350 in FlexiWork platform credit covering usage through 2026.
Will my staff actually use it?
Yes. The interface is intentionally simple — if your team uses WhatsApp, they can use FlexiWork. Most businesses see full team adoption within 48 hours, with no training sessions needed.
What's the difference between Rosta and the Platform?
Rosta is your internal scheduling system — managing your own permanent and part-time staff. The Platform connects you to FlexiWork's external verified gig worker network for shifts your internal team cannot cover.
When does the gig platform launch?
We're actively building it now. Beta businesses get first access and priority matching when it goes live — and the S$350 credit applies to gig platform usage too.
Do you support multiple locations?
Yes. FlexiWork Rosta handles multi-venue management from one dashboard. Cross-venue shift invites, duplicated rosters, and consolidated reporting — all from a single login.
How long is the beta, and can I leave?
Beta runs for several months minimum — you'll get plenty of notice before anything changes. And yes, you can leave anytime. No contracts, no credit card, no lock-in. We want you here because it works, not because you're stuck.
What happens after beta?
Beta businesses lock in grandfathered rates — permanently lower than what new customers will pay. You shaped how we built this; your pricing reflects that.
What if I need support during setup?
Direct email support during beta from the team. We want your feedback and we're actively invested in making sure every business gets set up properly. Reach us at info@joinflexi.work anytime.
